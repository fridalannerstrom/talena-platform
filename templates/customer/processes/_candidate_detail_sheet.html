Important nuance

What could be misinterpreted?

Careful does not mean slow.

A structured and quality-oriented profile should not automatically be interpreted as low pace. It may instead mean that the candidate works best when priorities are clear and quality expectations are explicit.

Lower change motivation does not mean low adaptability.

It may simply mean that the candidate prefers changes to be explained, planned and connected to a clear purpose.
